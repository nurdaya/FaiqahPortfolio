About Me

Hi! I'm Faiqah, a final-year IT Applications Development student at ITE College East with a focus on web development and UI/UX design. I'm skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and design tools like Adobe XD, Photoshop, and Illustrator. I've developed mobile app projects that solve everyday problems and completed a year-long internship at Singapore Land Authority, where I worked on geospatial data, QGIS, and represented the agency at key events. In 2023, I took part in an overseas exchange programme to Denmark, earning a Python certificate and gaining global insight. I'm passionate about creating digital experiences that blend tech and creativity, and I also enjoy drawing as a hobby.
